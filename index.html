Our Story

Born from
flames & dreams

Bhai G & Cafe was born from a simple belief: food cooked over real fire tells a story no oven can replicate. Our chef-founders traveled from Oaxaca to Kyushu studying live-fire techniques before bringing their craft home. Every dish carries that spirit: honest ingredients, patient technique, and a genuine love for the table.
